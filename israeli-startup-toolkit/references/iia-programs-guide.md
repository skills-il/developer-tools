# Israel Innovation Authority (IIA) Programs Guide

## Overview
- **Website:** innovationisrael.org.il
- **Budget:** ~1.8B NIS annually
- **Mission:** Support R&D and innovation in Israeli industry

## Programs

### 1. R&D Fund (Keren HaMechkar)
- **Stage:** Seed to Growth
- **Funding:** Up to 50% of approved R&D budget
- **Repayment:** Royalties at 3-5% of revenue from funded product
- **Duration:** 12-24 month R&D plan
- **Requirements:**
  - Israeli registered company
  - R&D conducted in Israel
  - IP owned by company
  - Technological innovation component
- **Application:** Online via IIA portal
- **Review:** Professional committee, 2-4 months
- **Approval rate:** ~40-50%

### 2. Tnufa (Early Stage)
- **Stage:** Pre-seed / Idea
- **Funding:** Up to 85% of budget
- **Max Amount:** 800K NIS
- **Repayment:** Royalties 3-5%
- **Requirements:**
  - Very early stage
  - Technological concept
  - Small team (often single founder)
- **Application:** Online, simplified process

### 3. Incubator Program
- **Stage:** Early stage
- **Funding:** Up to 85%
- **Duration:** 2-year program
- **How it works:**
  - Company joins approved incubator
  - Incubator provides workspace, mentoring, services
  - IIA funds R&D budget
- **Approved incubators:** Various across Israel

### 4. Binational Funds
| Fund | Partner | Max Funding | Focus |
|------|---------|-------------|-------|
| BIRD | USA | $1M | Joint US-Israel projects |
| CIIRDF | Canada | Varies | Canada-Israel R&D |

### 5. Multinational Programs
- **Horizon Europe:** EU framework program
- **EUREKA:** European R&D network
- **Bilateral agreements:** With 40+ countries

## Key Restrictions

### IP Transfer
- IP developed with IIA funding CANNOT be transferred abroad without approval
- Transfer fee: Up to 6x the grant amount received
- This is the most significant restriction for startups considering acquisition

### Royalties
- 3-5% of revenue from products developed with grant funding
- Paid until grant fully repaid (with interest linked to LIBOR/SOFR)
- Royalties apply even if product evolves significantly

### Manufacturing
- IIA prefers manufacturing in Israel
- Manufacturing abroad may trigger higher royalty rates
- Must notify IIA of manufacturing plans

### Reporting
- Semi-annual progress reports
- Financial reports on grant usage
- Final report at project completion

## Application Tips

1. **Emphasize innovation** - IIA wants technological novelty, not just a business model
2. **Clear milestones** - Define measurable R&D milestones
3. **Realistic budget** - Overly optimistic budgets get flagged
4. **Team credentials** - Strong technical team matters
5. **Market validation** - Show you understand the market
6. **IP strategy** - Clear plan for protecting intellectual property
7. **Resubmit if rejected** - Address reviewer feedback and try again
