# Israeli Tax Rates and Financial Constants (2025)

## Income Tax Brackets (2025)
| Annual Income (NIS) | Marginal Rate |
|---------------------|---------------|
| Up to 84,120 | 10% |
| 84,121 - 120,720 | 14% |
| 120,721 - 193,800 | 20% |
| 193,801 - 269,280 | 31% |
| 269,281 - 560,280 | 35% |
| 560,281 - 721,560 | 47% |
| Above 721,560 | 50% |

## Tax Credit Points (Nekudot Zikui)
- Value per point: 2,904 NIS/year (242 NIS/month)
- Israeli resident: 2.25 points
- Woman: +0.5 points
- New immigrant (year 1-1.5): +3 points
- New immigrant (year 1.5-2): +2 points
- New immigrant (year 2-3.5): +1 point

|-------------|--------------|-------------------|
| Up to 7,122 NIS/month | 0.40% | 2.87% |
| 7,123 - 49,030 NIS/month | 7.00% | 12.83% |

## Health Tax (Mas Briut) 2025
| Income Range | Rate |
|-------------|------|

| Above 7,122 NIS/month | 5.00% |

## VAT (Ma'am)
- Standard rate: 17%
- Eilat: 0% (tax-free zone)

## Keren Hishtalmut (Education Fund)
| | Employee | Employer |
|--|----------|----------|
| Salaried | 2.5% | 7.5% |
| Self-employed | Up to 4.5% of income | - |
- Tax-exempt after 6 years (up to ceiling)
- Ceiling: 15,712 NIS/month (2025)

## Minimum Wage (2025)
