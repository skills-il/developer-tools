# Israeli Business Presentation Conventions

## Pitch Deck Structure (Israeli VC Standard)
1. Cover slide (company name + one-liner)
2. Problem (pain point, market context)
3. Solution (product demo or mockup)
4. Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM with Israeli context)
5. Business Model (pricing in NIS, revenue streams)
6. Traction (users, revenue, growth metrics)
7. Team (founders, military service background is common)
8. Competition (comparison matrix)
9. Financials (3-year projection)
10. The Ask (funding amount, use of proceeds)

## Formatting Conventions
- Currency: ₪ or NIS (not ILS in presentations)
- Dates: DD/MM/YYYY
- Numbers: comma for thousands (1,000,000)
- Percentages: 15% (number before symbol)

## Color Palettes Common in Israeli Tech
- Startup Blue: #0066FF / #2196F3
- Innovation Purple: #7C3AED
- Growth Green: #10B981
- Israeli Blue (flag): #0038B8
- Israeli White: #FFFFFF

## Slide Design Tips
- RTL text alignment throughout
- 30-point minimum font for body text
- Hebrew text reads right to left -- animate accordingly
- Use Hebrew numerals only in formal/traditional contexts
- Logos and company names in English are acceptable as-is
